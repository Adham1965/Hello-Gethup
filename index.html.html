What's the Football

Football is a team game consisting of two teams, each team consists of eleven players, the technical staff, substitutes and the medical staff. The team contains the attack line, the midfield line, and the defense line. The match consists of two halves, each half forty-five minutes without overtime, and the team with the most goals wins. And if there is a tie between the two teams after the time has expired. We go into overtime, each of fifteen minutes. And if the tie remains on the field, we go to penalty kicks

Championships

Football is the most popular game in the world, so it is played in all continents, and every continent has its championships. On this page, we will talk about all these championships.

The first thing we should know is that there are clubs and national teams. Clubs have their own championships, and also national teams have their own championships. So let's start with the club championships first.

Clubs Championships

In every European country there is a different league, but what is similar between them is the number of teams that consist of twenty teams, and all these teams fight over points. For example, if a team wins a match in the league, it gets three points, and if it ties, it gets one point, but if it loses, it does not get Points. Within thirty weeks only, and after the end of all matches, there will be the final ranking of the teams, which is determined by the most number of points. Accordingly, the teams with the first positions are the ones who qualify for the Champions League and the European League in Europe or African Champions League which we will talk about them below.
